Donor Clubs

Want to Help several birds that come and go through the Sanctuary every year?


Birds in Flight Sanctuary would like to introduce Donor Clubs. Below you will find several general groups of birds that are rehabilitated and set free every year. The donation for the group you chose will go into the veterinary care and feeding of that specific group of birds while they are at the sanctuary. Birds in Flight Sanctuary rehabilitates hundreds of birds annually...and each of them needs the support of donors like you to be fly free once again. Donor Club Members will recieve a...


  • 5x7 Photograph of a bird currently being rehabilitated in the group you choose.
  • Certificate Suitable for Framing.
  • Donor Recognition on our Website.

Your Club Donation will be debited from your credit/debit card via paypal.com. To join a club simply press the "donate button" under the club of your choice.

Commemorable Releases

For that special moment in your life Birds in Flight will release abird to freedom. Remember a loved one, a wedding gift, anniversary,birthday or any other occasion.

You will receive a 5x7 photo of the bird just before it is released, a certificate of release, and a letter of why the bird was brought to the Sanctuary.
